FAQ

Common questions

Is it safe to download?

Yes. Clean Aim Tracker is distributed through the Microsoft Store, which scans all apps for malware and requires publisher verification. It runs entirely locally — no network connections are made.

Does it track my mouse when I'm not using it?

No. Mouse input is only captured during active drill sessions. When a drill is not running, the app does nothing in the background. You can verify this in Task Manager.

Where does my data go?

Nowhere. All session data is stored locally on your device in a private app folder. No data is sent to any server, ever. You can delete everything from the settings panel at any time.

Will it work with my game?

Clean Aim Tracker is a standalone trainer — it runs alongside any FPS game. The sensitivity recommendation engine supports CS2, Valorant, Apex, Overwatch 2, and more.

How is this different from Aim Lab?

Aim Lab shows you stats. Clean Aim Tracker coaches you. After every session you get specific feedback based on your personal history — what your weak area is, why, and exactly what to practice next.

What happens after 30 free sessions?

You keep access to all drills with basic stats. Pro features — AI coaching, trend analysis, and progression tiers — require a subscription. No sudden lockout, no pressure.
